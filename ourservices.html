Our Services

1.Scholarship Preparatory Training Program (SPTP).

The Scholarship Preparatory Training Program is a holistic initiative designed to equip students with the essential skills, knowledge, and resources necessary to compete for scholarships and pursue higher education. This comprehensive program encompasses a variety of activities, including academic enhancement, scholarship orientation, and English language training. By identifying available scholarship opportunities, providing clear application guidelines, and guiding students through the process, our innovative online platform empowers individuals to confidently apply for scholarships and gain access to higher education.

Training session during Ashesi application period with Sudanese students

Scholarship orientation sessions with applicants

2.Financial Support for Youth in Refugee and Internally Displaced Camps.

We recognize the financial barriers that youth in refugee and internally displaced camps often face when seeking higher education. Our organization assists these individuals by offering financial support to cover admission or application fees and equivalent certifications required for foreign educational institutions. By breaking down financial barriers, we aim to enable these young individuals to access educational opportunities and expand their horizons.

3.Mentorship and Coaching.

Our mentorship and coaching services connect young individuals with seasoned mentors who provide guidance, inspiration, and support on their personal and professional journeys. Through meaningful relationships, experienced mentors offer valuable insights and advice, fostering personal growth and empowering the youth to navigate their paths with confidence.

South Sudanese and Sudanese Mentorship session.

4.Social Entrepreneurship Initiatives:

SSYLNET empowers young entrepreneurs with the tools to create sustainable solutions that contribute to South Sudan's economic development. Our social entrepreneurship initiatives provide aspiring business leaders with the knowledge, resources, and support needed to launch impactful ventures that address local challenges and foster positive change.

Creating enterpreneurial opportunities

5.Networking and Exposure Opportunities.

We offer a diverse range of networking events and exposure opportunities, both in the virtual and physical realms. These platforms enable young individuals to broaden their horizons, establish valuable connections, and expand their opportunities. By connecting like-minded individuals and facilitating interactions with professionals and peers, we help the youth access a wider world of possibilities.

Refugees and IDPs network during Refugees and IDPs session at ALU campus Rwanda

6.Leadership Development.

Our core programs focus on nurturing ethical and transformative leaders who possess the skills, knowledge, and values required to drive positive change within their communities. Through targeted workshops, mentorship, and experiential learning, SSYLNET equips young individuals with the capabilities to make a meaningful impact. By tapping into the immense potential of the youth, we envision South Sudan becoming a beacon of hope and opportunity.

7.Advocating for Climate Change and Biodiversity Conservation.

At SSYLNET, we are dedicated to advocating for solutions to address climate change and the loss of biodiversity. Recognizing the urgency of these environmental challenges, we work tirelessly to influence policies, raise awareness, and drive positive actions within South Sudan. Through our efforts, we aim to foster a transformative wave of change that addresses these pressing issues and secures a sustainable future.

SSYLNET aims at advocating for solutions that address climate change in south Sudan and Africa as whole.

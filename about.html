About us

PROBLEM STATEMENT

SSYLNET passionately address sthe critical problem of young people being deprived of their potential in South Sudan. We refuse to accept a world where limited access to education, vulnerability, illiteracy, conflicts, economic hardships, leadership gaps, and lack of empowerment hinder their growth. With unwavering resolve, we break chains, empowering young minds to soar beyond their circumstances

OBJECTIVES OF SSYLNET

Scholarship Excellence: We strive to provide deserving young individuals with access to quality education through scholarship programs, enabling them to acquire knowledge and skills that will empower them to contribute to the nation's growth.
Social Entrepreneurship: SSYLNET supports innovative social entrepreneurship programs that enable young leaders to create sustainable solutions to local challenges, fostering economic growth and social progress.
Leadership Development: Through mentorship, workshops, and experiential learning, we aim to cultivate a generation of ethical leaders who are equipped to navigate challenges and lead with integrity.
Community Development: We are committed to engaging in community-driven projects that address local needs, contributing to the overall well-being and development of South Sudanese communities.
Advocacy and Policy Influence: SSYLNET seeks to play a pivotal role in shaping policy agendas, particularly in the context of climate change, by advocating for evidence-based solutions and amplifying the voices of young leaders.
Gender Equality and Women's Empowerment: We are dedicated to breaking down gender-based barriers and stereotypes, empowering women to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.
